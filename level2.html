10 Reasons to Love You

Intelligence

Your intelligence is phenomenal. I love talking about all kinds of subjects with you because you’re logical. You question me, you understand me, you challenge me, and you complete my ideas. You’re my little nerd, and your intellect is your most attractive trait. 🧠✨

Cuteness

You are the cutest thing in this universe. It’s even hard to say, measure, or truly understand your cuteness, but it captivates me. Looking at you, hearing your voice, or seeing anything you do makes me want to squeeze you, hug you, put you in my pocket, and keep you forever. You’re my little cute darling, and I just cannot handle this. 🫶✨

Blue Eyes

You have the most beautiful eyes I’ve ever seen. They were the first thing I noticed and the first feature that attracted me. Looking into your eyes brings me peace, hypnotizes me, and makes me want to forget the whole world and look only at them forever. You’re my blue-eyed treasure, and ik hou van yal blauwe ogen. 💙✨

Kindness

You are an extremely kind person. The softness of your voice alone is already enough, but together with your cuteness, the lightness with which you deal with people, and the constant smile on your face, it makes anyone like you. Having you by my side and knowing that you are kind and loving gives me confidence. You are my precious sweetheart, and your smile enchants me. 😊✨

Trustiness

You are super trustworthy. You are consistent and sincere. Being near you gives me a sense of safety and complete confidence—I trust you with my achievements and my frustrations. You are my safe haven, and you make me feel like I could go to the ends of the earth with my eyes closed by your side. 🌍💖

Adventure

You’re an explorer. You love to travel and discover, to challenge yourself and be challenged. You enjoy experiencing new things and always want to grow, to know new places, new cultures, new languages. You’re my adventurer with your adventure shoes, and I want to explore the whole world by your side. 🌍✨

Funny

You’re funny. Your sense of humor is unique and intelligent, and you know the difference between a joke and an opinion. You laugh at my silly jokes and come up with ones just as silly. You’re my idiot, and every day with you is a happy one. 😄❤️

Beautiful

You’re beautiful! Your eyes are incredibly important, but your smile, your hair, your gaze, your skin—everything about you is perfect. You’re my pretty princess, and looking at you captivates me! 👑💖

Slijmbal

You’re sweet. What can I say? I love being loved, I adore hearing that you love me, how much you love me, what you love about me, what you admire, and what excites you. All the other reasons are for who you are, but this one is for how you treat me, and I love that about you. You’re my Slijmbal, and with you, I feel like the most amazing man in the world. 💖

Similarity

I made a list of your qualities, the things I love about you, and the things that attract me the most.

But the truth is, what I love most about you is how much we’re alike.

Alike in thought, alike in temperament, alike in tastes, dreams, and desires

You’re my soulmate, my other half.

You’re perfect for me, and I’m perfect for you.
